Our Story

At Sal7ly Home Services, our story is all about providing exceptional customer service and high-quality workmanship. Our company was founded in 2010 by a group of experienced professionals who saw a need for a more customer-focused approach to home services. Our founders realized that many homeowners were frustrated with the lack of communication, transparency, and accountability from traditional home service providers. They set out to create a company that would put the customer first, every time. From the beginning, our focus has been on building strong relationships with our clients based on trust, honesty, and respect. We believe that open communication is key to providing the best possible service, so we make sure to keep our clients informed every step of the way.
